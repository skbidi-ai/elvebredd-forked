Support & Help

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
Click on "Sign Up" and fill in your details. You'll receive a confirmation email to verify your account.
How do I list a pet for trade?
Once logged in, go to "Create Listing" and fill in the details about your pet. Upload clear images and set your asking price or desired trades.
Is this site safe?
Yes, we use HTTPS encryption and take security seriously. Never share your password with anyone, and report suspicious accounts.
What payment methods do you accept?
We facilitate trades directly between users. Payment methods depend on individual agreements between traders.
How do I report a scam?
Please contact our support team immediately with details and evidence. We take fraud seriously and will investigate all reports.
